# Positioning notes

## Category

Local memory and permission layer for AI assistants.

## Primary promise

Future is the local memory and permission layer for AI assistants—import your history, retrieve cited context, and approve exactly what leaves your machine.

## Wedge

Import your ChatGPT history and project files into a private, cited, model-agnostic assistant.

## Differentiators

- citations and immutable context inspection, not opaque retrieval;
- reviewable memory lifecycle, not automatic permanent memory;
- whole-prompt redaction and exact approval, not a generic privacy toggle;
- local ownership with replaceable models, not another hosted chat silo.

## Claims to avoid

Do not claim zero risk, production readiness, encrypted storage, complete offline operation with external profiles, autonomous agency, perfect redaction, or support for integrations that do not exist.

## Activation metrics

Measure successful frozen installs, demo starts, first imported source, first cited answer, first opened citation, first approval/denial decision, seven-day returning workspaces, contributor issue claims, and merged external pull requests. Stars are a secondary discovery signal, not the operating metric.
