# Code of Conduct

Future welcomes contributors of different backgrounds and experience levels. Participation in project spaces requires respectful, constructive behavior.

## Expected behavior

- discuss ideas and code without attacking people;
- give specific, evidence-based feedback and accept correction gracefully;
- respect privacy, attribution, boundaries, and different levels of experience;
- make project spaces accessible and focused on the work;
- disclose conflicts of interest that could affect technical decisions.

Harassment, discriminatory language, threats, sexualized attention, doxxing, spam, impersonation, and sustained disruption are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through the contact path on their GitHub profile. Do not include sensitive personal details in a public issue. Maintainers may edit or remove content, issue a warning, temporarily restrict participation, or permanently ban participation depending on severity and pattern. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical, with conflicts of interest recused.

This policy applies in repository discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews, and project-related public spaces.
